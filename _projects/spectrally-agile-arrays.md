## Overview

The upper mid-band (FR3, roughly 7&ndash;24 GHz) is emerging as a key frontier
for next-generation wireless, balancing the coverage of sub-6 GHz with the
capacity of millimeter-wave. This project develops **spectrally agile
large-scale antenna arrays** that can operate flexibly across this wide band.
