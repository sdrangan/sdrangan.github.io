## Overview

As LEO mega-constellations and terrestrial networks increasingly share spectrum
in the upper mid-band, robust **co-existence** becomes essential. This project
develops methods for joint detection, channel estimation, interference nulling,
and anti-jamming across the satellite-terrestrial interface.
